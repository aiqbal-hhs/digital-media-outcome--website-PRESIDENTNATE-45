Dinosaurs of Jurassic World
THIS PAGE IS CURRENTLY UNDER CONSTRUCTION!

Jurassic World theme park, NZ is under construction currently and therefore we have no photographs of our dinosaurs in their exhibits.When the dinosaurs are housed in their

paddocks in New Zealand (once the park opens) we will have a team of 30 photographers go through the park and capture many breathtaking snapshots through the lenses of their cameras.

When these photographs have been taken they will then be put on a USB stick for which our photographers will do their part in extracting the best photographs from the

wide range of photos they took. Once the best photos have been selected they will be handed on to the media team who will code these images into this website.
