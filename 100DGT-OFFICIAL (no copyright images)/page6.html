Resorts/Hotels & Restaurants of Jurassic World

Once the construction of Jurassic world is complete our park will have 18 operational Resorts/Hotels to accommodate our guests on their visit to our park. Our Resorts and Hotels will offer our guests a wide range of rooms that they can book to stay in for their time in the park. Each room includes a: 65” TV with a wide range of channels, Chromecast, board games, cards and a wide selection of books. Our rooms even offer a ‘Dinosaur’ projector light!


Our room inclusions are: Complimentary Wi-Fi, Welcome packs and amenities for the kids, A nightlight for the kids, Netflix (Free of charge) and a bunch of games for the kids.


Room amenities: Free unlimited Wi-Fi, Nespresso machine, In room dining, In room movies, Refrigerator, Luxury amenities, Separate Bath and Shower, Self-controlled Air-conditioning, Alarm clock, Separate Kids bedroom and Lounge area and a Large king bed for the parents.

Once operational, Jurassic World will have 3 large Restaurants and 3 large Bars operating. Our future plans are to have 8 Restaurants and 8 Bars, a total of 16. Currently our Restaurants have been built, they are the: Stampede Steaks, Les Gigantes (French: “The giants”) and Jurassic Dining. Each restaurant will offer a wide selection of delicious foods cooked by some of the most famous chefs in the world, we “Spared no expense” - John Hammond (Father of Jurassic park - now Jurassic World). Once completed we will have 3 large operational bars and they are the: T-rex Shots Bar, Crystal Skull Bar and the Spino Shots Bar. Each Bar will offer our Adults (18+) a very large selection of top-shelf Alcohol..
