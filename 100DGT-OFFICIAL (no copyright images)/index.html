WELCOME TO JURASSIC WORLD!

Step into a world where science is brought to life through interactive exhibits that delight and educate. Study the birth, extinction and rebirth of the many dinosaurs that will be safely housed in New Zealand’s luxury resort. Connect with the past and visit the future with groundbreaking exhibits, larger-than-life interactive films and hands-on activities in our high-tech Samsung Innovation Center.


Jurassic World will be a fully functioning theme park in New Zealand, when opened to the public in 2030. The main attractions of the park are real-life living dinosaurs, cloned by Dr. Henry Wu.


Inspired by, and built over, the original park on Isla Nublar, Jurassic World in New Zealand will contain elements of a biological preserve, a safari, a zoo, and a theme park similar to Disneyland, Walt Disney World, SeaWorld Orlando, San Diego and San Antonio, Busch Gardens and all of Universal Studios' locations. The park, like the aforementioned parks, will function as a luxury resort with hotels, restaurants, nightlife and even a golf course. By 2031, we estimate that our park will host over 20,000 visitors each day, while its hours of operation are set to be from 8AM until 10PM.
