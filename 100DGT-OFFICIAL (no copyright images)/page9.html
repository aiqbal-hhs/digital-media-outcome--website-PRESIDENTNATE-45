"May I present you... the Indominus Rex. Our first genetically modified hybrid."

Our Jurassic world in New Zealand will be the first park in the world to exhibit our first genetically modified hybrid. Indominus Rex was made to be the biggest, scariest attraction in the new Jurassic World. Dr. Henry Wu was tasked with creating a hybrid dinosaur that would feature all the most powerful and frightening aspects of dinosaurs that had been brought back to life in prior years. Dr. Wu estimates the Indominus rex to be “fifty feet long when fully grown, BIGGER than the T-rex.”
