Rides & Attractions of Jurassic World

Jurassic World is the most advanced amusement park in the entire world, incorporating all the latest technologies. What we've made are living biological attractions so astounding, that they'll capture the imagination of the entire planet. Once Jurassic World comes online in New Zealand you’ll be able to come and experience the many wonderful attractions our park will have to offer. Listed below are some of the original Jurassic World attractions from our other parks that will be coming to New Zealand. Something special about the Jurassic World theme park in New Zealand is that it will exhibit and house our first genetically modified hybrid but that’s not all! Jurassic World, NZ will feature the first ever Gondola Lift.

GYROSPHERE VALLEY

Come and roll your way through an amazing interactive ride! The Gyrosphere lets you come up close to over thirty dinosaur species.

CRETACEOUS CRUISE

Our Cretaceous Cruise offers our guests up close views of our large Herbivores while rowing down the lake within the deep woods of New Zealand.

THE AVIARY

The Aviary is an attraction in the Jurassic World park that houses the park's pterosaur species: Dimorphodon and Pteranodon.

GALLIMIMUS VALLEY

Come and experience our herd of Gallimimus from the safety of our armed tour trucks that will tour the Gallimimus valley.

GONDOLA LIFT

Building in New Zealand gives us the perfect opportunity to build a Gondola Lift, one that’ll put the Gondola in Rotorua to shame.

T-REX KINGDOM

The T-rex feeding show is a perfect experience to see how this large predator devours its prey. Parents be aware, this show may be disturbing for smaller children.

MOSASAUR FEEDING SHOW

Come and experience a once in a lifetime opportunity to see our massive Mosasaurus feed on the so-called ‘king of the Ocean’, Great White shark.

COMING SOON TO JURASSIC WORLD!

New Zealand will be the first park in the world to exhibit our first genetically modified hybrid. Click image above for more info.

VELOCIRAPTOR PADDOCK

Come experience our Animal behaviorist; Owen Grady, tame the Velociraptors that once roamed our planet.
