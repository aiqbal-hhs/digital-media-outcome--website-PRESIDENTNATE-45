Management roles of Jurassic World

This page lists the most important employees that work for Jurassic World. Everyone listed below keeps the Jurassic World theme park operational so that guests from all over the world can come and experience the magnificent Dinosaurs that have been brought back to life.

Claire Dearing

Park Operation Manager

Claire Dearing holds the position of Park Operations Manager of Jurassic World.

Simon Masrani

Park Owner & CEO

Simon Masrani is the CEO of Masrani Global and is the owner of Jurassic world theme park

Dr. Henry Wu

Head Scientist

Dr. Henry Wu is Head scientist of genetics and the creation of all dinosaurs for Jurassic World

Vic Hoskins

Head of Security

Vic Hoskins is the Head of all Security and soldiers working for InGen and Masrani Global.

Zara Young

Personal Assistant of Claire Dearing

Zara Young is the personal assistant of Claire Dearing, the Park Operations Manager.

Katashi Hamada

Commander of the Asset Containment Unit (ACU)

Katashi Hamada serves as the Commander of the Asset Containment Unit (ACU) on Jurassic World.

Owen Grady

Animal behaviorist

Owen Grady is an animal behaviorist who was employed by Jurassic World as a Velociraptor trainer.

Barry Sembène

Animal behaviorist & Owen Grady's assistant

Barry Sembène is Owen Grady’s personal assistant and an animal behaviorist.
